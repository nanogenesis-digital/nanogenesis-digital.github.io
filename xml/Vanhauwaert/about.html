About Maud Vanhauwaert

Maud Vanhauwaert (1984) is a Belgian poet and writer. She has published multiple collections of poetry – including Het stad in mij, which offers an overview of her work as the city poet of Antwerp (2018-2019) and more – and the novel Tosca (2023). She logged the writing process of the poem ''t' for a thematic exhibition on 'beginning' at the literary heritage institution of Flanders, het Letterenhuis.

About the writing process

Vanhauwaert wrote the poem in five sessions. She started on 7 May, with an evening writing session (at 9.59 p.m.). On 9 May, she then recorded the second writing session at 12.47 p.m. The third writing session followed a day later, on 10 May at 11.08 p.m. On 12 May, the last day of writing, there were two writing sessions: the first at 7.13 p.m. and the second at 10.56 p.m. Most of the writing sessions therefore took place in the evening. The writing process took a total of 3 hours, 28 minutes and 31 seconds.
